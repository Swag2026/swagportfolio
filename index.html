ABOUT SWAG

A brand that defines a generation.

SWAG began in Jeddah in 2012 with one plain ambition: youth fashion at a world standard, at a price that respects the young Saudi wearing it — global streetwear, read through a local eye.

More than fifty million pieces later, SWAG is one of the Kingdom's largest suppliers of modern youth fashion, with a settled presence in Jeddah, Riyadh and Dammam. Premium where it counts, minimal by choice — streetwear at heart.

The Mark Logo & System

One mark, legible at any size, on any surface.

The SWAG mark is built from plain letterforms that need no decoration — it works as small as a woven label and as large as a storefront wall.

Type Wordmark
Colors Gold · Ivory
Clear Space 2× cap height
Usage Label · Store · Digital
01 / MARK SWAG EST. 2012 KSA

The wordmark sits on squared-terminal letterforms — a shape that reads the same whether it's embroidered on a cuff or cut from vinyl for a shopfront.

Gold is reserved for the mark and key details only; everything around it stays quiet, so the logo is always the loudest thing in the room.

A good logo survives being small.

The Craft Fabric & Fit

Fabric that lasts, a stitch you can trust, a fit that returns.

The difference a customer feels but rarely names is craft — the weight of the cotton, the density of the stitch, the way a hoodie sits after its tenth wash. SWAG spends its attention exactly there.

Focus Fabric & Fit
Stitch Precise · Durable
Check QC on every run
Promise Wash after wash
HOODIE · PREMIUM COMFORT HEAVYWEIGHT · BRUSHED

Anyone can print a graphic on a blank. Fewer can make the blank worth printing on. SWAG starts at the fabric — a hand and a weight chosen for the Saudi climate and the way young people actually wear their clothes: often, hard, and for years.

From there it is discipline. Even seams, reinforced stress points, a collar and cuff that keep their shape, a hem that hangs straight. Every production run passes a quality gate before it is allowed near a shelf.

A garment earns loyalty on its tenth wash, not its first wear.

The Presence Jeddah · Riyadh · Dammam

Three cities, one experience — on the floor and on the phone.

A brand is met, not read. SWAG's presence spans the Kingdom's three major cities — a considered retail experience in person, and a clean, bilingual one online.

Presence is not just how many doors a brand has — it is whether they all feel like the same brand. SWAG's three-city footprint is deliberate: enough reach to matter across the Kingdom, tight enough to keep every experience consistent, from the hanger to the checkout.

Reach means little if the tenth store does not feel like the first. Consistency is the real footprint.
